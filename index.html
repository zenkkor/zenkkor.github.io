Where things usually break.

A short list of the patterns I see most often when founders bring me in.

Vision faster than velocity

You have a roadmap and an engineering team. Cadence is slow, scope creeps, AI experiments never make it out of branches.

Stuck between stages

Prototype works, production breaks. You need someone who has lived through 0 to 1, 1 to 100, and the years that follow, without a six-month onboarding tax.

AI without a system

Every engineer uses AI tools differently. No shared playbook, no measurable lift, no clear path from a working demo to a working product.
